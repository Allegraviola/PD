What Types of PD are there?
Each personality disorder type has its own set of diagnostic criteria. You must meet
some of these criteria to receive a specific diagnosis. The minimum filling quantity is
different for different types. If you meet the criteria for more than one type, it may be
called mixed personality disorder.
